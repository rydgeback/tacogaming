    Bots

    We use TacoBot on our server which provides us loads of fun commands, when you join you will be PM'ed a list of commands.

    We also have TacoPoints, a karma system for Discord. You can upvote someone with @user <"^", "+1", or "up">, and give 10 of your own points with @user gild. You'll lose points for doing bad things, and get a reward for being the most active user at the end of the week.
